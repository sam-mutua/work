Survival Analysis

Course Purpose

To impart skills on estimation of transition intensities/survival rates

Course Outcomes

At the end of the course learners should be able to:

  • Apply principles of actuarial modeling in solving insurance problems

  • Apply statistical models in approximations and estimations

  • Determine the survival function and hazard function

  • Determine age dependent transition intensities
